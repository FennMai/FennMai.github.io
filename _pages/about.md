## Doing now

### [1. Real Time Smart Shopping Cart](https://fennmai.github.io/Real_Time_Embedded_Team_Project/) (Continuing development)
![alt text](assets/about/image.png)
- Universal Compatibility: The Smart Shopping Cart seamlessly integrates into any retail environment. Initially requiring a one-time adaptive environment scan and manual setup, it subsequently offers fully automated shopping assistance for various customers.
通用兼容性：智能购物车可无缝集成到任何零售环境中。最初需要一次性自适应环境扫描和手动设置，随后为各种客户提供全自动购物帮助。
- Real-Time Adaptive Response: Equipped with an advanced Lidar system, our Smart Shopping Cart detects and avoids obstacles. When encountering pedestrians, it stops promptly and recalculates a new route to bypass crowded areas, ensuring smooth navigation through the store.
实时自适应响应：我们的智能购物车配备了先进的激光雷达系统，可以检测并避开障碍物。当遇到行人时，它会迅速停车并重新计算一条新路线以绕过拥挤的区域，确保在商店中畅通无阻。
- Enhanced Shopping Convenience: Our cart streamlines the shopping experience with its integrated camera and powerful 4-axis robotic arm, enabling it to automatically retrieve targeted products based on their store coordinates. This feature significantly reduces the hassle of searching for items, providing unparalleled ease for customers.
增强购物便利性：我们的购物车通过其集成摄像头和强大的 4 轴机械臂简化了购物体验，使其能够根据商店坐标自动检索目标产品。此功能大大减少了搜索商品的麻烦，为客户提供了无与伦比的便利。
- User-Friendly Interface: Displayed on a responsive QT screen, the Smart Shopping Cart not only shows map and sensor data but also accepts voice commands, enhancing usability. Experience the future of shopping with our cart, where technology meets practicality, making shopping enjoyable and efficient.
用户友好的界面：智能购物车显示在响应式QT屏幕上，不仅显示地图和传感器数据，还接受语音命令，提高了可用性。使用我们的购物车体验购物的未来，技术与实用性相结合，使购物变得愉快和高效。
